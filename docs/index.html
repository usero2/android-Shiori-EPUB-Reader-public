Shiori

EPUB Reader for Android — designed for Thai readers

▶ Get it on Google Play ☕ Support

✨ Features

📚

Library

Manage your EPUB collection with 3 view modes — Thumbnail, List, and Detail. Tracks reading progress automatically.

📖

EPUB Reader

Smooth infinite-scroll navigation with a searchable chapter list. Resumes to the exact paragraph — even where TTS left off. Custom fonts, themes, and line height.

🔖

Bookmarks

Bookmark any paragraph and jump back from a searchable list. Bookmarked paragraphs are gently outlined in the reader.

📱

Floating Controls

Floating TTS overlay works over any app. Pinch to resize, snap to edge. Keep listening while browsing.

✏️

Text Replacement

Replace words before TTS or in the reader view. Supports 5,000+ entries and regular expressions.

🎯

Select & Translate

Tap any word — Copy, Translate (Google Translate in-app), Start TTS from here, or Add to Replacement.

🎨

Themes

Light, Dark, and Sepia themes. Adjustable font size, line spacing, and page margins.

🔊 🌐 🎵

Advanced Multi-language Text-to-Speech

Auto-detects Thai, English, and Chinese script within the same paragraph and switches voices seamlessly. Per-language Pitch, Speed, and Voice settings. Preview each voice before reading. Works fully offline with your device's TTS engine.

📲 Get the App

1

Install from Google Play

Open Shiori on the Google Play Store and tap Install.

2

Add your books

Tap the + button and pick an .epub file — or open an EPUB from any file manager with Shiori.

3

Read & listen

Tap a paragraph to start Text-to-Speech, or just read. Your position — and bookmarks — are saved automatically.
